Mediterranean Stuffed Zucchini

Description

This Mediterranean stuffed zucchini recipe is a delicious and healthy side dish that is perfect for any occasion. It is packed with flavor and nutrients, making it a great choice for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium zucchini, halved lengthwise
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomato

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Scoop out the flesh of the zucchini and place in a large bowl. Add feta cheese, tomato, parsley, mint, and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Mix well.
  3. Stuff the zucchini halves with the mixture. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with paprika. Place on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until the zucchini is tender and the filling is heated through, about 20 minutes.
